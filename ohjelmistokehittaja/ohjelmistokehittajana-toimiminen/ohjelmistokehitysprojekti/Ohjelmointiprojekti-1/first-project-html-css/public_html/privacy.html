2. Purposes and legal basis for processing personal data

Customer information is only processed in connection with reservation.

3.Categories of personal data to be processed, information content and sources of information

Personal information is obtained from the customer themselves when reservation a table.

4. Retention of personal data

Data concerning customer identification will be retained for as long as required by legislation. We will delete your information at your request.

5. Processors and recipients of personal data

Personal information will be processed by the employee handling your recervation. The restaurant can also pass on your information to the police at their request.

6. Data transfers outside the EU/EEA

Data will not be transferred to other countries.

7. Data protection principles and security of processing

Data is stored securely, but due to novice coders, there may be security vulnerabilities.

8. Rights of the data subject

  • Right of access and inspect your personal data
  • Right to rectification and right to erasure
  • Right to data portability, right to restriction of processing and right to object
  • Executing your rights

Requests to exercise any of these above-mentioned rights must be made in writing and be signed by the person making the request. Requests may also be made in person at the data controller’s office located at the address Robinsonintie 123 12345 Punainen. The request must include the following information:

  • first and last name
  • date of birth or Business ID
  • email address
  • phone number
  • home address
  • postal code and place of residence or business
  • date and place
  • signature
  • name in block letters
  • information whether the request concerns inspecting, rectifying, deleting or transferring data and/or objecting to processing or restricting processing
  • information on which register the request is targeted at (for instance newsletter or customer feedback system).
